    <h2>🔍 The Problem: The "Visibility Gap"</h2>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Status Quo:</strong> Sales reports are often siloed by brand or payment mode, leading to mismatched stock levels and missed regional trends.</li>
        <li><strong>Operational Pain:</strong> High variance in "Cost per Acquisition" due to unoptimized marketing on low-performing weekdays (e.g., Wednesdays).</li>
        <li><strong>Market Context:</strong> Competitive pressure in Tier 1 and Tier 2 cities requires instant pivoting between high-margin models and high-volume models.</li>
    </ul>

    <hr />

    <h2>🛠️ The Architecture: 3-Pillar Analytics</h2>

    <h3>Pillar 1: Revenue Governance</h3>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>KPI Orchestration:</strong> Real-time tracking of revenue and units to monitor brand health at a glance.</li>
        <li><strong>Model Benchmarking:</strong> Direct comparative analysis of top models (iPhone SE, OnePlus Nord, Vivo Y51) to identify market leaders.</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>Pillar 2: Consumer Behavioral Mapping</h3>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Payment Ecosystem Tracking:</strong> Monitoring the shift toward digital liquidity to optimize checkout experiences.</li>
        <li><strong>Sentiment Analysis:</strong> A Rating Funnel that correlates product quality with regional sales success.</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>Pillar 3: Spatio-Temporal Intelligence</h3>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Regional Heatmaps:</strong> Geospatial visualization of "High-Value Clusters" in Mumbai, Delhi, and Bangalore.</li>
        <li><strong>Temporal Slotting:</strong> Identifying "Peak Demand Windows" (Saturday/Monday) to align staffing and promotional spend.</li>
    </ul>

    <hr />

    <h2>📈 Strategic Impact & ROI</h2>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Inventory Velocity:</strong> 1.5x faster stock rotation by identifying underperforming brands in specific seasonal dips (April/Sept).</li>
        <li><strong>Channel Optimization:</strong> Reducing transaction friction by aligning payment method support with actual consumer preference.</li>
        <li><strong>CX Excellence:</strong> Utilizing customer rating distribution to trigger proactive service interventions in low-rating clusters.</li>
    </ul>
