## 🔍 The Problem: The "Visibility Gap"
* **Status Quo:** Sales reports are often siloed by brand or payment mode, leading to mismatched stock levels and missed regional trends.
* **Operational Pain:** High variance in "Cost per Acquisition" due to unoptimized marketing on low-performing weekdays (e.g., Wednesdays).
* **Market Context:** Competitive pressure in Tier 1 and Tier 2 cities requires instant pivoting between high-margin models and high-volume models.

---

## 🛠️ The Architecture: 3-Pillar Analytics

### 1. Revenue Governance
* **KPI Orchestration:** Real-time tracking of revenue and units to monitor brand health at a glance.
* **Model Benchmarking:** Direct comparative analysis of top models (**iPhone SE, OnePlus Nord, Vivo Y51**) to identify market leaders.

### 2. Consumer Behavioral Mapping
* **Payment Ecosystem Tracking:** Monitoring the shift toward digital liquidity to optimize checkout experiences.
* **Sentiment Analysis:** A **Rating Funnel** that correlates product quality with regional sales success.

### 3. Spatio-Temporal Intelligence
* **Regional Heatmaps:** Geospatial visualization of "High-Value Clusters" in Mumbai, Delhi, and Bangalore.
* **Temporal Slotting:** Identifying **Peak Demand Windows** (Saturday/Monday) to align staffing and promotional spend.

---

## 📈 Strategic Impact & ROI
* **Inventory Velocity:** 1.5x faster stock rotation by identifying underperforming brands in specific seasonal dips (April/Sept).
* **Channel Optimization:** Reducing transaction friction by aligning payment method support with actual consumer preference.
* **CX Excellence:** Utilizing customer rating distribution to trigger proactive service interventions in low-rating clusters.
